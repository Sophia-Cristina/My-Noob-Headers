Helping tools for SDL lib.
It uses:
#ifdef USE_SDL2
or
#ifdef USE_SDL3
In some of the codes, but not all.
Consequently, there is space for codes to use legacy coding.

'Audio' is where my main signal callback function is, those who found my repo from my music may know this already, so, well, there is where you find it!

'Graph' have tools to aid in making graphs, the main header may call other header and have universal tools.
the 'ysxSDLTrade.h' have tools to deal with market related information, for example, a class to plot market candles.
